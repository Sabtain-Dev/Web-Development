"The Night King" is a fictional character from the popular television series "Game of Thrones," which is based on George R.R. Martin's "A Song of Ice and Fire" novels. The Night King is the leader of the White Walkers, a supernatural and malevolent race in the series. He is known for his icy and menacing appearance, with blue skin and glowing blue eyes.
